How Weight & Food Works

Your complete weight management system with AI-powered insights

1. Log Your Weight & Food

Scan Scale Display: Point your camera at your scale's display and let AI automatically read your weight. Works with digital scales from any brand - no typing, no errors, just instant capture of the numbers on your scale.

Scan Food: Take a photo of your meal or snack and get instant nutritional analysis. AI identifies the food and provides calories, protein, carbs, fat breakdown, and portion estimates. Perfect for tracking what you eat alongside your weight.

Manual Entry: Prefer to type? Enter weight manually along with optional measurements like body fat percentage, BMI (auto-calculated), waist circumference, or any other metrics you track.

Rich Context: Add notes about your day - meals eaten, exercise completed, water intake, sleep quality, stress levels, menstrual cycle, medications, or anything that might affect your weight. Attach progress photos to visually document your journey alongside the numbers.

2. Track Progress

Visual Tracking: See your weight history displayed in beautiful charts and graphs. View trends over days, weeks, months, or custom date ranges. Color-coded indicators show progress toward your goals.

Smart Averages: Automatically calculated statistics include weekly and monthly averages that smooth out daily fluctuations, weight change rate, time-to-goal projections, and trend lines that show real progress beyond day-to-day variations.

Milestone Celebrations: The app recognizes and celebrates your achievements - first 5 lbs lost, 10% of goal reached, longest streak, and more. Stay motivated with visual progress indicators and achievement badges.

3. Discover Patterns

AI Analysis: Artificial intelligence analyzes your notes to identify what correlates with weight changes. Discover insights like "You lose more weight in weeks when you mention meal prep" or "Weight increases on weeks with less than 5 exercise sessions."

Personalized Insights: Get specific recommendations based on your unique data. AI might notice "Your weight drops 0.3 lbs more on weeks with morning workouts" or "Weekends show consistent 1 lb increases that normalize by Wednesday."

Interactive Chat: Ask questions about your data in plain English: "What's my average weight loss per week?" or "Show me all weigh-ins after cheat days." The AI understands context and delivers instant answers from your tracking history.

4. Reach Your Goals

Goal Setting: Set target weights with realistic timelines. The app projects when you'll reach your goal based on current trends and shows whether you're on track, ahead, or need to adjust your approach.

Professional Reports: Export comprehensive PDF or Excel reports for nutritionists, personal trainers, or doctors. Reports include weight graphs, BMI trends, body fat percentage changes, notes about diet and exercise, and statistical summaries perfect for professional consultations.

Apple Health Integration: Full bidirectional sync with Apple Health. Import weight from smart scales or other apps, and export all your WT data to HealthKit. Your weight data stays connected with your complete fitness and health ecosystem.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Camera Scan AI work?

Simply point your iPhone or iPad camera at your scale's digital display. The app uses advanced AI-powered camera recognition technology to automatically read your weight and populate the field. This eliminates manual typing errors and makes logging faster and easier.

How often should I weigh myself?

Most experts recommend weighing yourself once a week at the same time and day for consistency. Some people prefer daily weigh-ins to see the natural fluctuations. The app supports any frequency that works for you.

What is BMI and why does it matter?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight. It's calculated as: weight (kg) / height (m)¬≤. A BMI of 18.5-24.9 is considered normal, 25-29.9 is overweight, and 30+ is obese. The app automatically calculates and displays your BMI with color-coded indicators.

How do I measure body fat percentage?

Body fat percentage requires a body composition scale - a special scale that measures both weight and body composition using bioelectrical impedance. These are available from brands like Omron, Tanita, and Withings. Standard scales only measure weight.

Why does my weight fluctuate daily?

Daily weight can vary by several pounds due to water retention, salt intake, hormones, meal timing, and bathroom habits. This is completely normal. Focus on weekly or monthly trends rather than daily changes.

Can I import weight from Apple Health?

Yes. The app can import all your historical weight data from Apple Health with one tap. This includes weight tracked by smart scales, Apple Watch, or other fitness apps.

How does AI help with weight loss?

When you add notes to your weight entries (like "started keto" or "ate pizza"), AI analyzes these over time to show correlations. For example, it might show that weeks when you meal prep correlate with better weight loss, or that eating out frequently correlates with weight gain.

Can I track weight in pounds and kilograms?

Yes. The app supports both imperial (pounds, feet/inches) and metric (kilograms, centimeters) units. You can switch between them in settings.

Can I export my weight data?

Yes. Export your weight history as PDF charts, Excel spreadsheets, or JSON data. Share reports with your doctor, trainer, or nutritionist.

Is this a diet or meal tracking app?

No. Weight & Food focuses on tracking body measurements (weight, BMI, body fat). It doesn't track calories or meals. However, you can add notes about your diet to help identify what affects your weight.

Do you show ads or track me?

No. We've completely removed all advertisements from the app. We don't track you across other apps or websites, and we never sell your data to advertisers.

Your data syncs via iCloud - just like Apple Notes and Photos. This means we don't need to know your email address, name, or any personal information. Your weight and health data is stored on your devices and in your iCloud account, and it's completely private whether you use the free version or premium.

Do I need a subscription?

The app is completely free to download and use. All core tracking features work without a subscription. A premium subscription unlocks advanced AI features, unlimited exports, and enhanced customization. You can try all premium features during the free trial period.
