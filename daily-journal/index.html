How Daily Journaling Works

Your personal journaling companion for capturing life's moments and discovering patterns

1. Write Your Thoughts

Instant Entry: Open the app and start writing immediately. No setup, no configuration, no complexity. Just your thoughts and the page. The clean, distraction-free interface helps you focus on expressing yourself.

Rich Media: Enhance entries with photos, attach images to capture memories visually. Add voice recordings, sketches, or any media that helps tell your story. Every entry becomes a complete snapshot of your day.

Note Templates: Use pre-made templates for daily reflections, gratitude journaling, goal tracking, dream logs, or create your own custom templates. Templates provide structure while still giving you freedom to write naturally.

2. See Patterns Emerge

Sentiment Analysis: AI automatically detects the emotional tone of your entries - positive, negative, or neutral. See your mood trends visualized over weeks and months to understand your emotional patterns.

Word Frequency: Discover which topics, people, or themes you write about most. The app identifies recurring words and phrases, revealing what's consistently on your mind and how your focus shifts over time.

Calendar View: Browse your entire journaling history in a calendar interface. Each day shows an excerpt and mood indicator. Quickly jump to any past entry to see what you were thinking on that day.

3. Reflect and Grow

AI-Powered Insights: Ask questions about your journal: "What were my happiest days last month?" or "Show me all entries where I mentioned my goals." Get instant, thoughtful answers that help you understand your journey.

Personal Growth Tracking: Review how your thoughts, concerns, and aspirations evolve. See progress toward goals mentioned in past entries. Identify growth areas and celebrate how far you've come.

Search Everything: Powerful search finds any word, phrase, or concept across years of journaling. Filter by date, mood, or keywords. Your entire written history is instantly accessible and searchable.

4. Keep Everything Safe

Private & Secure: All entries are encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account. Only you can read your journal - the app uses end-to-end encryption so your words stay completely private.

Automatic Backup: Weekly automatic JSON backups ensure you never lose your thoughts. Manual backup anytime to export your entire journal. Your words are protected against device loss or failure.

Cross-Device Sync: Write on iPhone, continue on iPad, review on Mac. All your entries sync automatically via iCloud. Your journal follows you everywhere, always up-to-date on every device.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my journal private?

Absolutely. Your entries are stored encrypted on your device and in your private iCloud account. We never see your writing. Even the AI sentiment analysis happens entirely on your device using Apple's Natural Language API - your words never leave your phone.

How does sentiment analysis work?

When you write 8 or more words, the app uses Apple's built-in Natural Language framework to analyze the emotional tone. An emoji appears to show whether the entry reads as positive, negative, or neutral. This happens entirely on-device for complete privacy.

What is word frequency analysis?

The Trends feature analyzes all your entries to show which words and topics you write about most often. This helps you identify recurring themes, concerns, or interests in your thinking.

Can I add photos to my journal?

Yes! You can attach up to 8 photos to each journal entry. Photos are stored efficiently with automatic thumbnail generation, and they sync across all your devices via iCloud.

What are note templates?

Templates are pre-written prompts that help you start writing quickly. Create custom templates for things like gratitude lists, daily reviews, goal tracking, or any other recurring format you use.

Can I export my journal entries?

Yes. You can export your entries as PDF, Excel, or JSON files. This lets you create backups, share selected entries, or move your data to other platforms if needed.

Does it work on Mac?

Yes! The app runs natively on Apple Silicon Macs (M1 and later). Your entries sync automatically between your iPhone, iPad, and Mac via iCloud.

Can I write multiple entries per day?

Absolutely. Write as many entries as you want each day. Some people journal in the morning and evening, while others add notes throughout the day.

Do you show ads or track me?

No. We've completely removed all advertisements from the app. We don't track you across other apps or websites, and we never sell your data to advertisers.

Your data syncs via iCloud - just like Apple Notes and Photos. This means we don't need to know your email address, name, or any personal information. Your journal entries are stored on your devices and in your iCloud account, and they're completely private whether you use the free version or premium.

Do I need a subscription?

The app is completely free to download and use. All core tracking features work without a subscription. A premium subscription unlocks advanced AI features, unlimited exports, and enhanced customization. You can try all premium features during the free trial period.
