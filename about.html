Technology & Features

Artificial Intelligence Capabilities

All Feeltracker apps are powered by advanced AI technology:

  • Camera Scan AI (OCR): Automatically reads values from device screens - blood pressure monitors, weight scales, and other health devices
  • Conversational Chat Assistant: Ask questions about your health data in natural language
  • Automatic Insight Generation: AI analyzes your data and generates personalized insights
  • Pattern Detection: Identifies correlations between lifestyle factors and health metrics
  • Sentiment Analysis: Understands emotional tone in journal entries
  • Text-to-Speech: Listen to insights and summaries

Apple Health Integration

Comprehensive bidirectional integration with Apple HealthKit:

  • Read: Import historical health data from Apple Health
  • Write: Automatically save new entries to HealthKit
  • Supported Data Types: Blood pressure, heart rate, weight, body fat percentage, BMI, sleep analysis, mental health (iOS 18+ State of Mind)
  • Automatic Sync: Background synchronization across all iOS devices

Privacy and Security

  • End-to-End Encryption: All health data is encrypted
  • iCloud Storage: Data stored only in user's private iCloud account
  • No Server Storage: Data never passes through external servers
  • Complete Ownership: Users have full control and ownership of their data
  • GDPR Compliant: Meets European data protection standards
  • No Data Selling: User data is never sold or shared with third parties

Export and Sharing

  • PDF Reports: Professionally formatted reports for healthcare providers
  • Excel Spreadsheets: Detailed data exports for analysis
  • JSON Files: Complete data backups for migration and archival
  • Doctor-Friendly: Reports include summary statistics, trend charts, and detailed logs
