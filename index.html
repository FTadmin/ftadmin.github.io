Common Questions

Do you show ads or track me?

No. We've completely removed all advertisements from all Feeltracker apps. We don't track you across other apps or websites, and we never sell your data to advertisers.

Your data syncs via iCloud - just like Apple Notes and Photos. This means we don't need to know your email address, name, or any personal information. Your health data is stored on your devices and in your iCloud account, and it's completely private whether you use the free version or premium.

Do I need a subscription?

All apps are completely free to download and use. Core tracking features work without a subscription. A premium subscription unlocks advanced AI features, unlimited exports, and enhanced customization. You can try all premium features during the free trial period.
